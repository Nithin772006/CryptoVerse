About CryptoEdge

CryptoEdge was born from a simple idea: Cryptocurrency prices should be transparent.

We realized that different exchanges sell Bitcoin and other coins at slightly different prices. This creates an opportunity called Arbitrage.

Our mission is to provide a beginner-friendly platform where anyone can see these price differences in real-time and make smarter trading decisions.
